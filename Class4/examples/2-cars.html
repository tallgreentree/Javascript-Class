A Tale of 2 Cars

Mike and Sam decided to race their cars, so they each hopped in their autos.

BTW, Mike's favorite swear is "FEATHERS!" and Sam's is "GEESH!"

Sam got off the line first, so Mike got mad.

Mike floored it, speeding past Sam a little bit later.

As the course was short, Mike won the race. Both drivers slowed to a stop. Being a good sport, Sam didn't swear again.
